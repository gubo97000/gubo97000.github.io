Who am I?

🎓 Study-wise I am a traveling student of computer science at Aalto University, culminating my Master Studies in Human-Computer Interaction and Design in the joint university program of EIT Digital. My first year of master was in Politecnico di Milano, Italy. My bachelor’s degree was from the University of Catania, my hometown.

👨‍💻 Professionally I have mainly been a front-end developer, working on MVP for the startup loop.ai. My passion for video games landed me a job as an HDR gameplay curator for Rehjong Tech. and LG Display, which spun off into coding and user test interface in Python. I want to see myself as a jack of all trades, and master of some (in the future).

🎮 Hobby-wise I am a hobby coder, a comic/manga reader, an animation fan, and a video game aficionado. I always love a good bike ride or a hike in nature.
